# Consumer Electronics

> The **consumer electronics industry** produces electronic devices designed for personal or household use. Products range from communication devices and entertainment systems to wearable technology and small household electronics. This industry is part of **light manufacturing** and relies on components from electrical equipment, semiconductors, and software industries.
