# Display Devices Industry

> The **display devices industry** focuses on the design and manufacture of visual display technologies, including flat-panel displays, OLED screens, LCDs, and related components. These products are widely used in consumer electronics, computers, televisions, and industrial applications. The industry is part of **light manufacturing** and closely linked to semiconductor, electronics, and consumer electronics sectors.
