# Advertising Industry

> The **Advertising Industry** creates, plans, and delivers marketing communications designed to promote products, services, organizations, or ideas. It serves businesses, governments, and nonprofit entities by shaping consumer perception, stimulating demand, and enhancing brand value. Advertising operates across traditional and digital media platforms, integrating creativity, psychology, data analytics, and strategic planning.
