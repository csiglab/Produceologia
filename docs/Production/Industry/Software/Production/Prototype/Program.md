> **Prototyping** involves the pursuit of iterative **refinement and experiential learning**, embracing the principle that the **essence of a design or idea** is best revealed and **enhanced** through **continuous cycles of creation, evaluation, and evolution**.
> 

> Prototyping is a software development approach where a simplified version of a system or product is created for testing and validating design concepts.
