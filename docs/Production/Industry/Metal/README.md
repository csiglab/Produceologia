# Metalworking Industry

> The Metalworking Industry transforms raw metals into finished or semi-finished products through processes such as cutting, shaping, forging, stamping, casting, machining, and welding. This industry serves construction, transportation, machinery, and consumer markets.

## Product Space

| Product                    | Description                                                          | NAICS Code |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| Forged & Stamped Products  | Metal components formed through forging or stamping processes        | 3321       |
| Fabricated Metal Products  | Structural metals, metal containers, fasteners, and assemblies       | 332        |
| Hand Tools & Hardware      | Hammers, wrenches, screws, bolts, hinges, and other small hardware   | 3322       |
| Machined Components        | Custom metal parts produced via machining, turning, or milling       | 3327       |
| Sheet Metal Products       | Cut, bent, or stamped sheet metal used in construction and machinery | 3323       |
| Metal Castings             | Metal parts produced via sand, die, or investment casting            | 3315       |
| Industrial Machinery Parts | Metal parts used in machinery, engines, and equipment                | 333        |
