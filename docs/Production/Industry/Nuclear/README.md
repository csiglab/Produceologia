# ‚ò¢Ô∏è Nuclear Industry

> The **Nuclear Industry** encompasses the **production, conversion, and utilization of nuclear materials and energy**, including power generation, fuel cycle management, research, and applications in medicine, industry, and defense. It integrates physics, engineering, and complex safety systems to harness nuclear fission (and increasingly fusion) for electricity, propulsion, and scientific advancement. As a **strategic and high-technology industry**, it demands rigorous regulation, specialized infrastructure, and advanced materials science.
