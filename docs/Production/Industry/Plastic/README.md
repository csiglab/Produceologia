# Plastics Products Manufacturing

> The **plastics products industry** produces goods made from synthetic polymer materials (thermoplastics and thermosets). Products range from household containers and packaging films to toys, industrial components, and consumer goods. This is a **light manufacturing industry** closely related to rubber products manufacturing and other polymer-based sectors.
