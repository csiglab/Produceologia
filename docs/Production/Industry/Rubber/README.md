# Rubber Products Manufacturing

> The **rubber products industry** produces a wide range of goods made from natural or synthetic rubber. Products include hoses, seals, belts, gaskets, mats, tires, and other molded or extruded rubber items. This is a **light manufacturing industry** closely related to plastics manufacturing and other polymer-based industries.
