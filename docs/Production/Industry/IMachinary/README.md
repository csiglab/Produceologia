# Industrial Machinery Industry

> The Industrial Machinery Industry manufactures machines and equipment used in industrial processes, including production, material handling, automation, and specialized manufacturing. This industry serves a wide range of sectors, from manufacturing and construction to agriculture and energy.

## Product Space

| Product                                 | Description                                                                             | NAICS Code      |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Machine Tools                           | Equipment used to shape, cut, or form materials in manufacturing                        | 333512          |
| Industrial Robots                       | Robots designed for manufacturing and automation processes                              | 333249          |
| Conveyors & Material Handling Equipment | Systems for moving, storing, and controlling materials in production or warehousing     | 333924 / 333921 |
| Pumps & Compressors                     | Machinery for moving fluids or gases in industrial processes                            | 333911 / 333912 |
| Specialized Manufacturing Equipment     | Custom machines for food processing, packaging, or other industry-specific applications | 333999          |
