# Real Estate

## Taxonomy

| **Level** | **Category**     | **Instance**                   | **Description**                                                                                   |
| --------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1         | Product          | Single-family home             | Most granular unit; individual residential property directly produced, bought, or sold.           |
| 1         |                  | Office unit                    | Specific commercial space; individual asset for sale or lease.                                    |
| 1         |                  | Warehouse                      | Specific industrial/storage building; tangible asset.                                             |
| 2         | Product Group    | Residential Properties         | Groups all residential units (e.g., homes, apartments, condos).                                   |
| 2         |                  | Commercial Properties          | Groups office spaces, retail buildings, and other commercial assets.                              |
| 2         |                  | Industrial Properties          | Groups warehouses, factories, and industrial buildings.                                           |
| 3         | Industry         | Residential Development        | Firms building, selling, or managing residential properties.                                      |
| 3         |                  | Commercial Property Management | Firms managing or leasing commercial properties.                                                  |
| 4         | Industrial Group | Real Estate & Construction     | Aggregates industries related to development, management, and construction of properties.         |
| 5         | Sector           | Construction & Property        | Major segment of the economy covering multiple industrial groups in property development.         |
| 6         | Major Sector     | Services-Producing Industries  | Highest-level aggregation; covers all service-oriented sectors including construction & property. |
