# ü™® Nonmetallic Mineral Product Manufacturing Industry

> The **Nonmetallic Mineral Product Manufacturing Industry** produces goods made from minerals that are **not metals or fuels**, such as glass, cement, ceramics, clay, and lime. These materials are essential for construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure, serving as foundational inputs for buildings, roads, and industrial equipment. The industry combines chemical processing, thermal treatment, and shaping techniques to transform raw mineral feedstocks into durable and specialized products.
