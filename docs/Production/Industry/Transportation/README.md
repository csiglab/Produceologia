# Transportation Equipment Manufacturing

> The Transportation Equipment Manufacturing Industry produces vehicles, vehicle components, and transportation machinery used in personal, commercial, and industrial transportation. This industry serves both consumer and industrial markets and includes land, rail, air, and marine transportation equipment.
