# Footwear Manufacturing

> The **footwear industry** produces shoes and related leather or synthetic products for daily wear, sports, formal occasions, and specialty uses. Footwear manufacturing is a **light industry** that often uses textiles, leather, rubber, and plastics as inputs. It sits downstream of material suppliers and upstream of retail distribution.
