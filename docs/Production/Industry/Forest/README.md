# Forest Sector

> The Pulp Industry produces cellulose pulp from wood, recycled paper, or other fibrous materials. Pulp is the primary raw material for paper, cardboard, tissue, and some specialty cellulose products. This industry is considered upstream in the broader Pulp and Paper sector.

> Which Industries Comprise the Forest Sector?

| **Timber Production & Logging**           | Harvesting of trees for lumber, veneer, and other raw wood products.                             | 113210          |
| **Sawmills & Wood Preservation**          | Primary processing of logs into lumber, boards, and treated wood.                                | 321113          |
| **Wood Panel & Engineered Wood Products** | Manufacturing plywood, particleboard, MDF, and other engineered wood products.                   | 321211–321219   |
| **Pulp & Paper**                          | Producing pulp, paper, cardboard, and related paper products.                                    | 322110–322121   |
| **Furniture Manufacturing**               | Production of wooden furniture and other wood-based household items.                             | 337110–337122   |

| **Forestry Services**                     | Consulting, certification, forest inventory, GIS mapping, ecosystem monitoring.                  | 541620 / 115310 |
