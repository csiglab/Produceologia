# Paper Industry

> The Paper Industry converts cellulose pulp into paper, cardboard, tissue, and other paper products. This industry is downstream of the Pulp Industry and produces the materials used in packaging, printing, and various consumer and industrial applications.

## Product Space

| Product                  | Description                                                     | NAICS Code      |
| ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Printing & Writing Paper | Paper used for books, office supplies, notebooks, and printing  | 322121          |
| Newsprint                | Paper used for newspapers and similar publications              | 322122          |
| Tissue Paper             | Paper for hygiene, facial, and household uses                   | 322122          |
| Cardboard & Paperboard   | Paper-based boards for packaging and structural applications    | 322211 / 322219 |
| Specialty Paper          | High-performance paper for industrial or technical applications | 322219          |

## Related Industry(s)

| Industry           | Description                                                                  | NAICS Code      |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Pulp Industry      | Produces raw cellulose pulp used to make paper products                      | 322110 / 322121 |
| Packaging Industry | Uses paper and paperboard to manufacture packaging materials                 | 322211 / 322219 |
| Printing Industry  | Uses paper products for printing books, newspapers, and commercial materials | 323110 / 323120 |
| Recycling Industry | Supplies recovered paper as feedstock for recycled paper production          | 562920          |
