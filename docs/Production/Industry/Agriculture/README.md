# Agriculture

> Agriculture is extremely broad—so it is best understood as a sector composed of a system of industries, each with its own technologies, production models, market structures, labor systems, and value chains.

> **Industry**: A set of firms producing similar goods or services using similar technologies or processes.

> **Market**: A social and institutional mechanism for exchange between buyers and sellers of a given good or service.

## Sub Sector Set

> Which are the sub sectors (industry groups) that compose agriculture?

| Sub Sector                                                                         | Description                                                                                           |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Crop Production (Arable & Permanent Crops)**                                     | Cultivation of annual and perennial plants for food, feed, fiber, fuel, and industrial raw materials. |
| **Livestock Production**                                                           | Breeding and raising animals for meat, milk, eggs, hides, wool, and genetic material.                 |
| **Mixed Farming Systems**                                                          | Integrated crop–livestock systems where land, nutrients, and capital are jointly allocated.           |
| **Plant Propagation & Nurseries**                                                  | Production of seedlings, grafts, ornamental plants, and planting materials.                           |
| **Aquaculture**                                                                    | Controlled cultivation of aquatic organisms (fish, mollusks, crustaceans, aquatic plants).            |
| **Agroforestry & Silvopastoral Systems**                                           | Integrated tree–crop–livestock systems combining forestry and agriculture.                            |
| **Forestry & Logging** *(often classified separately but biologically contiguous)* | Cultivation and harvesting of timber and non-timber forest products.                                  |
| **Apiculture & Small Biological Systems**                                          | Production of honey, wax, pollination services, and other small-scale biological outputs.             |
