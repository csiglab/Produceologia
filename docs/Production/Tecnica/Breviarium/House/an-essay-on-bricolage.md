## Formulation

> What characterizes bricolage as a technical field?

> Bricolage can be characterized as a practice-oriented technical field centered on the adaptive use, combination, and transformation of available materials, tools, and techniques to solve practical problems in situ. Unlike formal engineering disciplines, bricolage operates under conditions of constraint, contingency, and partial specification, emphasizing pragmatic effectiveness over theoretical optimality.
