# Research Methods(s)

| **Research Domain**         | **Primary Data Sources**                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | **Analytical Methods**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Product Space**           | • National accounts (Input–Output, Supply–Use tables) <br>• Trade and customs data (HS, SITC, BEC classifications) <br>• Firm-level microdata (production, exports, balance sheets) <br>• Industrial and sectoral surveys                   | • Product space and economic complexity analysis <br>• Input–Output linkage and multiplier modeling <br>• Productivity decomposition (TFP, labor/capital shares) <br>• Structural change and upgrading modeling <br>• Network and cluster metrics           |
| **Service Space**           | • Labor and employment surveys <br>• Service registries (finance, logistics, ICT, education, health, tourism, etc.) <br>• Platform economy and digital services datasets <br>• Occupational and task databases (ISCO, ESCO, O*NET)          | • Productivity and value-added decomposition <br>• Service system design and mapping <br>• Labor reallocation and task dynamics modeling <br>• Input–service linkage modeling <br>• Intangible capital measurement                                          |
| **Technology Space**        | • Patent and bibliometric databases (WIPO, USPTO, EPO, Scopus) <br>• R&D and innovation surveys (CIS, OECD STI indicators) <br>• Startup and venture capital datasets <br>• Technology readiness and diffusion indices                      | • Patent citation and technological distance analysis <br>• Capability and innovation network modeling <br>• Technology diffusion and absorptive capacity models <br>• R&D causal inference <br>• Evolutionary and systems-of-innovation modeling           |
| **Production Organization** | • Firm registries and ownership structures <br>• Corporate financial and operational data <br>• Business network and supply-chain transaction data <br>• Institutional and governance datasets (legal forms, regulation, industrial policy) | • Organizational and inter-firm network analysis <br>• Governance and ownership modeling <br>• Agent-based and system dynamics simulations <br>• Comparative institutional analysis <br>• Organizational learning and coordination modeling                 |
| **Foundations**             | • Energy and material flow accounts (SEEA, IEA, EXIOBASE) <br>• Environmental-economic datasets (resources, emissions, water, land use) <br>• Infrastructure, logistics, and spatial data (GIS, WDI, UNEP)                                  | • Energy–economy–environment (E3) modeling <br>• Material flow and circular economy analysis <br>• Ecological input–output and footprint models <br>• Resilience and sustainability indices <br>• Integrated system-of-systems simulation                   |
| **Finance Space**           | • Financial accounts and balance sheet data (national, sectoral, firm-level) <br>• Investment and venture capital flows <br>• Credit, monetary, and capital market statistics <br>• Development finance and public investment datasets      | • Financial network and flow-of-funds analysis <br>• Capital allocation and intermediation modeling <br>• Macroeconomic–financial linkage analysis <br>• Simulation of financial architectures <br>• Development finance and investment efficiency analysis |
