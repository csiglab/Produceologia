# **An Essay on Production Characterization**

> A concise framework to describe **what is produced**, **how it is produced**, and **which organizational structures** enable that production.

## Formulation

> **What is the product being produced?**

> Define the product space: nature of the output, core function, value proposition, and essential attributes.

## Production Model

> **How is the product produced?** What is the teche?

> Describe the production logic: processes, transformations, inputs, technologies, constraints, and flow of value creation.

## Firm Organization Model

> **Which organizational structure implements this production model?**

> Specify the roles, coordination mechanisms, governance structures, and operational subsystems that sustain production.
